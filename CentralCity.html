Central City

Central city is the capital of Amestris as well as the location of the main military command base. It is the most populated city in Amestris and the structure is such that every road leads to Central Command.

Characters

Edward Elric

The “Fullmetal Alchemist.” The protagonist in the series, Edward Elric and his brother attempted human transmutation to try and bring their mother back to life. In doing so, he lost his left leg. He then lost his right arm when he bonded his brother’s soul to a suit of armor. He enrolls in the State Alchemist program to gain access to their research as well as receive funding to try and return his and his brother’s bodies to their original state. During the series, he uses automail, a form of prosthetic. Because he has seen The Truth, he is able to use alchemy without a transmutation circle.

Alphonse Elric

Edward’s little brother. He has no physical human body, but his soul is bonded to a suit of armor. Because of this, he never eats or sleeps. He is extremely gentle and kind and loves picking up stray animals he finds on their adventures and putting them in his armor. He is also a skilled alchemist and expert fighter, but only learns to use alchemy without a transmutation circle later in the series. He is often mistaken for his brother, with the name “Fullmetal Alchemist.”

Roy Mustang

Colonel Roy Mustang is a young Military officer who practices Flame Alchemy. His abilities were used during the Ishvalan War of Extermination, an event which he Never forgot or forgave himself for. He values loyalty and Is a bit of a ladies’ man and is often seen lazing around his office talking to his dates.

Riza Hawkeye

Riza Hawkeye is Roy Mustang’s right-hand woman and bodyguard. She is the daughter of Roy’s deceased Flame Alchemy teacher. She is fiercely loyal to Roy while also acting as his babysitter from time to time. She is transferred to the Fuhrer’s office to act as his secretary in the later episodes. She is an expert marksman.

Maes Hughes

Maes Hughes was a Lieutenant Colonel in the Amestrian Military working in the Investigations Department. He was caring and well-liked by everyone that knew him, As well as a husband and father. His best friend Was Colonel Roy Mustang and his death was the catalyst for the story to begin happening in Central City.

Alex Louis Armstrong

Major Alex Louis Armstrong is the eldest son of the Armstrong family. He practices Strong Arm Alchemy as a State Alchemist. His abilities were also used in the Ishvalan War of Extermination, where he attempted to save two Ishvalan women from their execution. Unable to handle the mass murders he was being forced to commit, his commander sent him away from the battlefield. He sees this as one of his ultimate failures in his life, and subconsciously uses his physical strength to make up for that fact. He is very proud of his physique, often taking his shirt of to flex. He is one of the smartest allies the Elrics have. His sister, Olivier, is the commander of the Briggs Fort, and together they take down the homunculus Sloth.

Maria Ross

Originally assigned to watch over the Elrics while in Central, 2nd Lieutenant Maria Ross becomes a sort of mother figure to the Elrics, often protecting them and objecting to Edward’s often childish behavior. She is framed for Maes Hughes’ murder and is believed to be killed by Roy Mustang in retaliation, but it is discovered later that he saved her life before her execution. She is an ally to Mustang’s forces when they attempt to take over Central in the later episodes.

Ling Yao

Ling Yao is a prince from Xing. He is 12th in line to the throne of the emperor. In order to win the throne for his clan, he comes to Amestris seeking the secret to immortality. He finds it in homunculi, and his body is merged with a Philosopher’s Stone to make the new version of Greed, often called Greeling. He becomes friends with the Elrics, much to Ed’s chagrin. His will is strong enough to sometimes overpower Greed and talk to others as himself. He is accompanied to Amestris by his bodyguards Fu and Lan Fan.

Father's Lair

The base of the antagonists of the series. The tunnels under Amestris all lead back to here. It is directly under Central Command.

Characters

"Father"

The Dwarf in the Flask: Homunculus. The original Homunculus, Father creates the other homunculi and uses them as his minions. He does not leave his underground lair under Central command. The main antagonist for the series, he believes that sacrificing all of Amestris in a giant transmutation circle will elevate him to a perfect being, or “God.” He bears a striking resemblance to the Elrics’ father, as his blood was used to create him initially.

King Bradley/Wrath

Fuhrer King Bradley, revealed later to be the Homunculus Wrath, is the leader of Amestris and controls the military. He has super speed and strength, as well as incredible swordsmanship. He keeps his eye hidden under an eyepatch, as that is where his Ouroboros tattoo is. He has a wife, a woman who he was able to choose himself, but claims he has no attachment to her, as he is not human.

Selim Bradley/Pride

Selim Bradley, revealed later to be the Homunculus Pride, uses his child form as a container for him to move around in. In reality, he is a shadow like figure that can produce tendrils and sharp teeth as well as eyes. He guards the tunnel under Amestris. As the name suggests, he shows great pride and selfishness, never showing remorse or being helpful to his fellow homunculi.

Greed

A homunculus that doesn’t usually associate with the others, Greed wanders around Amestris doing anything he wants, usually seeking money or sex. He befriends some chimeras and makes a bar their base of operation as he searches for ways to get back at Father. Eventually, original Greed is absorbed back into Father and New Greed takes over Ling Yao’s body, becoming Greeling. He is often sarcastic and detached from other people’s affairs but grows to respect Ling as they share a body.

Lust

The homunculus in a stereotypically attractive woman form, she is the mastermind of many of the homunculi plans. She is the first homunculus to be killed, burned to death by Roy Mustang after he finds out her role in the death of Maes Hughes.

Gluttony

Father’s failed attempt at making a Gate of Truth, Gluttony is a sometimes docile homunculus who solely thinks about eating. He has improved senses to track his food, which can be anything from buildings and trees to humans. His stomach can never be filled, as it is an endless chasm between the world and Truth. He often follows Lust around and takes a liking to her.

Envy

A homunculus that can change into the form of any living being it sees; Envy was the one who killed Maes Hughes disguised as Maria Ross. He has a deep hatred of humans and a more outspoken personality than the other homunculi. He can also transform into a giant green monster with the faces of all the souls inside him somewhere on his body, and, when weakened, becomes a small, worm like creature.

Sloth

Sloth is a large, workhorse type of homunculus who digs the tunnels under Amestris. He is incredibly strong but also very slow, physically and mentally. He is often outsmarted by Olivier Armstrong when he appears aboveground in Briggs. He is killed by the Armstrong siblings.
